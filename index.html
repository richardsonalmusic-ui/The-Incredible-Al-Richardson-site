About

Dublin singer–songwriter · alt / indie rock

Dublin singer–songwriter “The Incredible Al Richardson” blends Mod roots, ’90s British guitar influences, and classic storytelling to craft a sound shaped by decades of musical discovery.

After emerging on the Dublin scene in the 2000s and returning to writing in 2023, he has recorded a new collection of tracks — including Man Made, featuring a standout guitar performance from Ocean Colour Scene’s Steve Cradock.

With fresh material planned for 2026 and a renewed presence on the live circuit, Richardson steps back into the spotlight with a voice that’s both seasoned and unmistakably his own.

Watch this space for new releases, gigs, and stories from the road.
